Access https://static-jep-digitaljlearning.pantheonsite.io/taxonomy/term/7/all?sort_by=created&sort_order=DESC en We Don't "Do Computers" Anymore! https://static-jep-digitaljlearning.pantheonsite.io/blog/2013/05/13/we-dont-do-computers-anymore-0 <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>During the mid to late 1990s I was teaching for a public school district.  I’m often shaken awake – usually during an important meeting – by the vivid memory of marching my students to the computer lab once a week.  The lab was equipped with enough outdated Apple computers – later replaced by repurposed Macs – for the entire class to practice mouse, keyboard and occasional math skills.  In other words, they mostly played games for 45 minutes and most of these games were about as academically rigorous as eating the pizza they served in the cafeteria.</p> <p>Fortunately that model evolved with time to one that, in theory at least, supported the work teachers and students were doing in class.  However, in many places, the once-a-week trip to the “school arcade” was transformed into 45 minutes of teacher prep and schmooze time while a media specialist or an educational-technology teacher  babysat under the official guise of helping with research for a project.</p> <p>Back in those days technology was seen as a separate entity from “real academics.”  We “did computers” once a week. Technology purchases were driven by extra money, not academic need.  Fortunately for all, times have changed and we’ve evolved once again.  Unfortunately, many of us missed the memo.</p> </div></div></div> <div class="field field-name-taxonomy-wp-blog-tags field-type-taxonomy-term-reference field-label-inline clearfix"> <div class="field-label">Tags:&nbsp;</div> <div class="field-items"> <span class="field-item even"><a href="/blog-tags/blended-learning" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Blended Learning</a>,</span> <span class="field-item odd"><a href="/blog-tags/mobile-labs" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">mobile labs</a>,</span> <span class="field-item even"><a href="/blog-tags/access" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Access</a>,</span> <span class="field-item odd"><a href="/blog-tags/computer-labs" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Computer Labs</a>,</span> <span class="field-item even"><a href="/blog-tags/computers" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">computers</a>,</span> <span class="field-item odd"><a href="/blog-tags/educational-technology" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Educational Technology</a></span> </div> </div> Mon, 13 May 2013 15:02:16 +0000 ghartstein 53 at https://static-jep-digitaljlearning.pantheonsite.io
