Description

The Charles E. Smith Jewish Day School is an independent community day school serving students from Kindergarten through 12th grade. The School is dedicated to creating an environment in which students can grow to their fullest potential as responsible and dedicated members of the Jewish community and of American society. This is their first year providing online course offerings.

Why They Chose DigitalJLearning

Online learning programs aid in the advancement of technology integration at Charles E. Smith Jewish Day School. These programs have facilitated opportunities for students to take ownership of their own learning. Bringing online learning to Charles E. Smith will help expand curriculum, and allow students to take courses that would otherwise be unavailable due to staff restrictions, low enrollment, or lack of time in the traditional school-day schedule.
