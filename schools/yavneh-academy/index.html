Description

For more than 60 years, Yavneh Academy, a modern orthodox yeshiva day school, has provided students with a superior educational foundation built on Torah Sh’bichtav, Torah Sh’BaalPeh, mitzvot, middot, Ivrit, EretzYisrael, MedinatYisrael, and excellence in all Judaic and general studies. The school has been using online learning for 4 years through NJ Virtual School and distance learning in Israel.

Why They Chose DigitalJLearning

On-line learning affords many opportunities to customize student studies. While teachers are highly trained individuals working to ensure the best education for each child, on-line learning offers a plethora of additional learning opportunities, inherently promoting a differentiated approach.Yavneh has integrated technology into its curriculum on an on-going basis. They have partnered with the New Jersey Virtual School to offer advanced courses in science and math for students who needed to step out of their current classroom curriculum. These courses allow for personal academic growth. In addition, Yavneh has benefited from distance learning with teachers in Israel by “bringing” them into the Tanach classrooms through Skype. This type of integrating on-line learning allows students to benefit from educators throughout the world with specialties not available within the school's walls. They are continuously looking for academic advancement.
